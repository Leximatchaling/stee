New Barangay Health Center Opens

Residents gain better access to healthcare

A new barangay health center was opened this week to provide better medical services for residents. The facility offers basic health checkups, vaccination programs, and maternal care.

Local officials say the health center will reduce travel time for residents who need medical assistance. Many citizens expressed gratitude for the new facility.



Local School Wins Science Competition

Students show talent in innovation

Students from the local high school recently won a city science competition with their innovative project. The team developed a simple water filtration system using affordable materials.

The project impressed judges because it focuses on helping communities access clean drinking water.
