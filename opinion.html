Editorial

Working Together for a Better Community

One important issue in our community is waste management. Many streets still experience problems with garbage collection and improper disposal.

I believe that solving this issue requires cooperation from both residents and local officials. Citizens should practice proper waste segregation while the barangay should provide more garbage bins and regular collection services.

If everyone works together, our community can become cleaner and healthier. Let us all participate in keeping our environment safe for future generations.
