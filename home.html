BREAKING NEWS: Marikina marks World Kidney Day 2026

Marikina marks World Kidney Day 2026

Mayor Maan Teodoro joined residents and health advocates in commemorating World Kidney Day 2026 in Marikina City, highlighting the importance of kidney health and preventive care. This year’s observance carried the theme “Kidney Health for All: Caring for People, Protecting the Planet,” which encourages greater awareness and action toward protecting kidney health.

In his message, Mayor Teodoro emphasized that Marikina’s culture of care begins with individuals taking responsibility for their own health and well-being. He also reminded the public that kidney disease can affect people of any age and is often detected only when it has already progressed. To prevent kidney-related illnesses, the mayor urged residents to maintain a healthy lifestyle by eating properly, drinking enough water, exercising regularly, and seeking regular medical checkups.
